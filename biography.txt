Jean Feng is an Associate Professor in the Department of Epidemiology and Biostatistics at the University of California, San Francisco and the UCSF-UC Berkeley Joint Program in Computational Precision Health. She is a principal investigator at the UCSF-Stanford Center of Excellence in Regulatory Science and Innovation (CERSI) where she collaborates closely with researchers from the US Food and Drug Administration to develop methods that improve the safety and reliability of AI/ML-based medical devices. Jean is also the data science lead on the PROSPECT team, the digital innovation taskforce for the Zuckerberg San Francisco General Hospital. She completed her Ph.D. in Biostatistics under Noah Simon and Erick Matsen at the University of Washington.
